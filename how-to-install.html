Installation Guide

Follow these 3 simple steps to install the game on your Android device.

Prepare your Device

Go to your phone's Settings > Security or Privacy. Look for "Install apps from unknown sources" or "Unknown Sources" and enable it. This allows you to install APK files downloaded outside the Play Store.

Download and Install

Go to our Download Page and get the APK file. Once downloaded, tap on the file in your notification bar or file manager to start the installation.

Open and Play

Once installation involves finishing, you will see a success message. Tap "Open" to launch the game. Enjoy your unlimited coins and gems!

Important: If you have the original version of Hungry Shark Evolution installed from the Play Store, you MUST uninstall it first before installing this MOD version. Otherwise, the installation will fail with an "App not installed" error.
